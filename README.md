# <p align="center">😸CATalyst😸</p>
## <p align="center">The CATalyst for your emotions</p>

<p align="center">This is a website created for cat lovers and all those who might be even the slightest bit intrigued about cats, to explore a world of different cat breeds. </p>

<br>
<br>
<br>

## <p align="center">✨Features✨</p>

- Browse detailed information about many cat breeds (origin, temperament, energy level, stranger friendly etc.)
- Add/remove breeds from your personal **Favourites** list (saved with localStorage)
- Dedicated Favourites page with additional images
- Responsive design tested across many devices
